## Frameworks Principais por Consultor

### Alex Hormozi (CL141)
- **Grand Slam Offer**: Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood / Time x Effort
- **Core Four**: Leads (warm outreach, cold outreach, content, paid ads)
- **Value Equation**: Maximize resultado + probabilidade, minimize tempo + esforco
- **Frase**: "Make people an offer so good they feel stupid saying no."

### Russell Brunson (CL143)
- **Value Ladder**: Free → Front-end → Middle → Back-end → Continuity
- **Perfect Webinar**: 3 Secrets framework
- **Attractive Character**: Igor = Reluctant Hero
- **Epiphany Bridge**: Storytelling para vendas
- **Dream 100**: Identificar 100 pessoas que ja tem sua audiencia
- **Frase**: "You're always just one funnel away."

### Erico Rocha (CL144)
- **Formula de Lancamento**: CPL (3 videos pre-lancamento)
- **Lancamento Semente**: Para quem tem zero audiencia
- **6em7**: R$100K em 7 dias como meta
- **Frase**: "6 em 7: R$ 100 mil em 7 dias."

### Seth Godin (CL146)
- **Minimum Viable Audience**: Menor publico viavel
- **Purple Cow**: Ser notavel
- **Permission Marketing**: Ganhar permissao, nao interromper
- **Frase**: "Marketing is no longer about the stuff that you make, but about the stories you tell."

### Dan Kennedy (CL151)
- **Magnetic Marketing**: Message-Market-Media
- **Price is story**: Preco e narrativa, nao numero
- **Frase**: "Price is a story, not a number."

### Jay Abraham (CL152)
- **3 Ways to Grow**: Mais clientes x Maior ticket x Maior frequencia
- **Strategy of Preeminence**: Seja tao bom que ir para outro e burrice
- **Risk Reversal**: Garanta o resultado
- **Host-Beneficiary JVs**: Parcerias estrategicas
- **Frase**: "There are only 3 ways to grow a business."

### Naval Ravikant (CL154)
- **Specific Knowledge**: Encontre sua intersecao unica
- **4 Leverages**: Labor, Capital, Code, Media
- **Frase**: "Arm yourself with specific knowledge, accountability, and leverage."

### MJ DeMarco (CL153)
- **CENTS**: Control, Entry, Need, Time, Scale
- **Frase**: "CENTS: Control, Entry, Need, Time, Scale."
